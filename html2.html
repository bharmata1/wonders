The Seven Wonders of the World

  1. Chichen Itza

    2. Chichen Itza was a large city built by the Mayan civilization in the early 400s A.D.

    It is located on the Yucatan Peninsula in Mexico and is considered to be one of the most influential cities ever.

    It is one of the most visited locations in Mexico with several million tourists visiting each year.
